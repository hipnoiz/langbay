About me

I offer professional English-Polish translation services supported by Master’s studies in English Philology and 5 years of experience in translating. I work with many translation agencies and individual clients from Poland and around the world.

My areas of specialization:
  • Law
  • Business, trade
  • Marketing, market research
  • Internet, e-commerce, mobile apps

I keep up with the latest technology solutions that facilitate the translation process, such as CAT tools (Computer Aided Translation). I use SDL Trados Studio 2014 which allows me to create translation memories and termbases. This makes me speed up my work and ensure consistent terminology across projects.

I assure:
  • Timeliness
  • Commitment
  • Reliability
  • High quality
